Privacy Policy

Operator: Changhan Ahn (affiliation: Korea Polytechnic University, Daegu Campus, Battery System Dept.) · v1.0 · Effective 2026-06-09

This policy applies to the MAIA self-diagnosis, report, and consultation service and takes effect from the effective date. Personal data is collected only when you request a report or consultation; the self-diagnosis (score check) is anonymous.

Changhan Ahn (Korea Polytechnic University, Daegu Campus, Battery System Department; "we"/"Operator") processes personal data for the MAIA AI Applicability Diagnosis service in line with this policy, GDPR (where applicable), and Korea's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA).

  • We do not collect special categories (health, biometric, ideology, etc.).
  • The 8-question self-diagnosis itself is anonymous. Personal data is collected only when you choose to request the report.

2. Purposes of Processing

  1. Producing and sending you the AI applicability diagnosis report.
  2. Scheduling and providing consultation about the diagnosis result.
  3. (If you opt in separately) Sending follow-up information, case studies, and resources related to the diagnosis.

We do not process data for any purpose other than the above.

3. Retention Period

  • We retain personal data for up to 1 year after the diagnosis / consultation purpose is fulfilled, then delete it.
  • We delete promptly upon a deletion request from you.
  • Where law requires longer retention, that law overrides.

4. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose your personal data to any third party.

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data.
  • (EU/EEA residents) Right to data portability and right to lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority.
  • How to exercise: send a request to the Privacy Officer email below. We will act without undue delay.

7. Deletion Procedure

  • Electronic files: permanently deleted in a non-recoverable manner.
  • Printed materials (if any): shredded or incinerated.
  • Targets: Google Sheet rows, local lead data (JSON), generated diagnosis report files.

8. Security Measures

  • Operational account separation (dedicated operations email and spreadsheet); minimum-necessary access.
  • Secrets and credentials managed via environment variables only (never in source, docs, or commits).
  • Lead file names and storage locations are managed so personal data is not exposed in external directories.
